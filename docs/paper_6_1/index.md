# Paper 6.1 – Operational Addendum

Paper 6.1 is the operational companion to Paper 6.  
Where Paper 6 defines the invariants and convergence properties at the theoretical level,  
Paper 6.1 binds those invariants to concrete substrate components:

- The **MRS engine** in `simulation/mrs/`
- The **CTS layer** in `compliance/cts/`
- The **reflexive monitoring and convergence surfaces** exposed to operators

This section documents how the theory is realized in code, how to run the examples,  
and how to extend the mappings for new environments and decision surfaces.
