# Paper 6.1 — Design Principles

This document outlines the design principles guiding the operational
implementation of Paper 6.

## Principles

### 1. Minimalism
Only essential structures are implemented.

### 2. Reflexivity
The system must observe itself without destabilizing.

### 3. Continuity
Meaning must persist across surfaces.

### 4. Governance
All operations must respect constraints.

### 5. Extensibility
The system must support future layers (e.g., Paper 7).
