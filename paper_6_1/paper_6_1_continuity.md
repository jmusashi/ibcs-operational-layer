# Paper 6.1 — Continuity Engine

This document describes the continuity engine (M3) implemented in
`mapping_continuity.py`.

## Purpose
Continuity binds surfaces, invariants, and reflexive loops into a coherent
operational field. It ensures that meaning persists across surfaces and time.

## Continuity Events
A continuity event includes:

- Surface
- Action
- Payload

These events form the continuity chain.

## Continuity Engine
The engine records events and provides access to:

- Full history
- Last event
- Cross-surface transitions

This is the operational heart of Paper 6.1.
